Our Purpose

GreenPrint was born as the culmination of a visionary final project at Harvard's CS50x course, driven by a shared commitment to harnessing technology for positive change. Beyond academia, GreenPrint emerged as an initiative to better our world. Our purpose extends beyond the mere transformation of plastic bottles into 3D printing filament; it's about transforming possibilities, fostering sustainability, and leaving a lasting impact on our planet.

Eco-Friendly

The GreenPrint machine exemplifies eco-friendliness through its transformative process. By repurposing discarded plastic bottles as the raw material for 3D printing filament, it actively reduces plastic waste.
